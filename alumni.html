Alumni Association Inauguration -School of Engineering



   The Computer Engineering Department is extremely glad to invite all its Alumni right from the First Batch 2014 to the Most Recent Batch 2020 for the Online Alumni Meet 2021. The Alumni Meet will be conducted on Microsoft Teams Platform on 14th August 2021 Saturday from 2.00 PM to 4.00 PM. “Multimedia Quiz Competition for Alumni” will be the main attraction of this Meet. The Winners of the game will get Cash Prizes/Amazon Gift Vouchers. The Alumni can join the meet as Participant for the Quiz Competition or as Audience. Along with the Quiz Competition, there will be discussions, experience sharing, guidance, interaction with Juniors etc. Please fill the following form to register for the Alumni Meet and get a Collection of 46 Popular English and Marathi E-Books in response. Please note that you can mark your entry as Quiz Participant only till 1st August. But the form will remain active till event day to welcome all our alumni. Meet your Friends, Batch mates, Professors and Juniors!! Re-live Old Memories… Share… Cherish…Enjoy!!!
